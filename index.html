沉浸式舞蹈·空即是色

Immersive Dance·nothing is something

作品介绍

Introduction of the work

空即是色，我们随意的动作看似漫不经心、毫无实义，但在深层次的理解后，可能暗藏玄机。书法是我们选择的表现形式，舞蹈与书法之间有相通之处。我们用身体去感受，用舞姿去表达，去挖掘表象之下的实质内容，去体会舞蹈与书法的联系。
我们的作品包含视频和线下体验空间两方面。以视频的形式呈现我们的概念，剖析舞蹈与书法的相似相连之处。打造一个结合光影、视听的沉浸式体验空间，进行简单的交互，感受舞蹈和动作可能呈现的样子。

Nothing is something.0 Our casual actions seem to be careless and meaningless, but after deep understanding, probably hides mystery. Calligraphy is the form of expression we choose. There are similarities between dance and calligraphy. We use the body to feel, dance to express, to excavate the substance under the surface, to experience the connection between dance and calligraphy.
Our works include video and offline experience space. Present our concept in the form of video, and analyze the similarities between dance and calligraphy. Create an immersive experience space that combines light, shadow and audio-visual, and make simple interaction to feel the possible appearance of dance and action.

创作媒介：摄像、ps、pr、processing、kinect
用ps写下水墨书法，摄影记录舞蹈创作，再通过视频剪辑制作舞蹈与书法融合的效果，最后利用processing做互动效果，打造沉浸式体验空间。

Creative media: camera shooting, PS, PR, processing, kinect
Ink calligraphy was written with PS, dance creation was recorded by photography, and then the fusion effect of dance and calligraphy was produced through video clip. Finally, processing and kinect were used as interactive effects to create an immersive experience space.

作品背景

Background of the work

现状调研：
多地美术馆、音乐厅已经采用沉浸式舞蹈表演，沉浸式舞蹈将数字美术和舞蹈融入一体，给观众带来奇妙的视听体验。
经过调查，68%的人在观看艺术作品中喜欢沉浸式体验，其中有84%的人认为沉浸式体验能够带来与众不同的感受。

Current situation survey:
Museums and concert halls in many places have adopted immersive dance performances, which integrate digital art and dance to bring wonderful audio-visual experience to the audience.
According to the survey, 68 percent of people like immersive experience when watching art works, and 84 percent of them think immersive experience can bring different feelings.

创作动机：
我们想给观众带来沉浸式体验，不仅是视听上的，还能亲身参与，通过交互设计给观众带来别致的体验。在对身体表达和深层次理解后，来体悟“空即是色”的道理。

Motivation:
We want to bring an immersive experience to the audience, not only audio-visual, but also hands-on, through interaction design to bring unique experience to the audience. After the expression and deep understanding of the body, we realize "nothing is something" .

设计目标

Objectives of the design

 

内容传达：
沉浸式体验空间，不仅是视听上的，还能亲身感悟。这个空间就是属于观众的空间，起兴由你，变幻由你，淡漠由你，手起手落间便能让这里瞬息万变。一扫就是书法的撇，一挥就是书法的捺，一切都由你来创造。

The immersive experience space is not only audio-visual, but also personally felt. This space belongs to the audience. It is up to you to rise, change and vanish. It can change by your hand and figure. A sweep or a weave is calligraphy, everything is created by you.

思考启发：
空即是色，我们随意的动作看似漫不经心、毫无实义，但在深层次的理解后，可能暗藏玄机。书法只是我们表现的一种可能，舞蹈与书法之间有相通之处，既随性又不失框架，既洒脱又不失理性。书法之外可能蕴含的其他概念，就要每个人在生活中自行探索了。

Nothing is something, our casual action looks like careless, without real meaning, but after deep - seated understanding, probably hides mystery. Calligraphy is only a possibility of our performance. There is something in common between dance and calligraphy, which is casual but also in order, free but also rational. It is up to everyone to explore other concepts beyond calligraphy.

服务对象：
想要开拓对身体的想象、对舞蹈意义的理解的人。

People who want to develop an imagination of the body, or an understanding of dance.
