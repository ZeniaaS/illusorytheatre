解构重构：
舞蹈是身体表达的一种形式，我们把舞蹈作为我们研究身体的媒介。以光影媒介作为载体，与身体相辅相成。以摄影的方式表现身体韵律，以书法作为衍生对象，让身体舞姿与书法相结合。
现代科技与传统艺术文化发生碰撞、产出火花，“沉浸式”体验应运而生，科技让艺术作品可以不再局限于不再单一感官的表现，而升华为一场全沉浸式的感官盛宴，达到全身心的融入、沉浸和情感交流，体会舞姿与书法之间的关系。

Deconstruction and reconstruction:
Dance is a form of body expression, and we use dance as a medium through which we study the body. The light and shadow are used as the carrie. The body rhythm is presented in the way of photography, and the calligraphy is combined with the body dance as the derived object.
Modern technology and traditional art culture collision sparks, "immersive" experience arises, science and technology make art can no longer confined to a single performance of the senses, and sublimate is a fully immersive sensory feast, to understand the relationship between the dance and Chinese calligraphy.

转译设计：
用舞蹈来演绎书法，再利用processing和kinect进行人像捕捉，识别特定姿势，生成对应笔画或书法图案，进行再创作。

Translational design:
Dancing deduces calligraphy, at the same time, processing and Kinect are used to capture figure, identifying specific postures, and generating corresponding strokes or calligraphy patterns for re-creation.
